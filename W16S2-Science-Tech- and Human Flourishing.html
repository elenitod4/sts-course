02 Core Concept

What is Human Flourishing?

Eudaimonia (Aristotle)

The state of living well and doing well. Not mere happiness but the fulfillment of one's purpose and potential.

Modern View

Physical health, mental wellbeing, meaningful relationships, creative expression, and contribution to society.

07 Pillar 1

Intentional Technology Use

Practical Steps:

  • ⏰ Set screen time limits (Digital Wellbeing / Screen Time)
  • 🔕 Turn off non-essential notifications
  • 📵 Phone-free zones (bedroom, dining table)
  • 🧹 Curate feeds: unfollow what drains you
  • 🎯 Ask: "Am I using this tool, or is it using me?"

  • • Empower, not control

12 Seligman's PERMA Model

PERMA × Technology

P - Positive Emotion ✅ Entertainment, music apps ❌ Comparison, doomscrolling
E - Engagement ✅ Creative tools, games (flow) ❌ Passive scrolling kills flow
R - Relationships ✅ Video calls, messaging ❌ Shallow connections, ghosting
M - Meaning ✅ Volunteer platforms, causes ❌ Nihilism from info overload
A - Achievement ✅ Goal-tracking, online courses ❌ Procrastination, distraction

5 Questions · Let's go!

Q1 Quiz

What is "Eudaimonia"?

A) A Greek word meaning "happiness only"
B) Living well and fulfilling one's purpose
C) The pursuit of wealth and material comfort
D) A type of ancient Greek technology
✅ B) Living well and fulfilling one's purpose — Aristotle's concept of human flourishing that goes beyond mere pleasure or happiness.
Q2 Quiz

Which is an example of tech HINDERING flourishing?

A) Khan Academy providing free education
B) Telemedicine connecting rural patients to doctors
C) Infinite scroll design on social media
D) GCash enabling financial inclusion
✅ C) Infinite scroll design on social media — Intentionally designed to steal attention and create addictive behavior patterns.
Q3 Quiz

What does PERMA stand for?

A) Positive Emotion, Engagement, Relationships, Meaning, Achievement
B) Progress, Education, Resources, Money, Access
C) Personal, Economic, Religious, Material, Academic
D) Passion, Energy, Resilience, Mindfulness, Ambition
✅ A) — Martin Seligman's scientifically validated framework for measuring human flourishing.
Q4 Quiz

What is Amartya Sen's Capability Approach?

A) Technology should be given equally to everyone
B) Flourishing = freedom to do and to be, not what you possess
C) GDP is the best measure of human progress
D) Capabilities are determined by your social class
✅ B) — Sen argues that development and flourishing should be measured by the capabilities (freedoms) people have, not just resources.
Q5 Quiz

Why is the Digital Divide a threat to flourishing?

A) It makes internet too expensive for companies
B) It only affects people in other countries
C) It's a temporary problem that will fix itself
D) It prevents equal access to tech-enabled opportunities
✅ D) — ~40% of Filipinos lack reliable internet, meaning they can't access education, healthcare, or economic opportunities that tech provides.
