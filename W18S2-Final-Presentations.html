04 Pro Tips

What Gets You an A 🏆

✅ Do This:

  • • Analyze, don't just describe
  • • Connect to Filipino context specifically
  • • Show multiple perspectives (who benefits? who's harmed?)
  • • Propose actionable recommendations
  • • Engage the audience (questions, polls, demos)
  • • Cite credible sources

❌ Avoid This:

  • • Reading everything from slides
  • • Copy-paste from Wikipedia
  • • Only one perspective (tech is great! or tech is bad!)
  • • No Filipino context
  • • Going over time (instant deduction)
  • • One person does all the work

06 Q&A Tips

How to Handle Q&A

For Presenters:

  • 👂 Listen to the full question first
  • 🤔 Pause and think before answering
  • ✅ "Great question!" (buys you time)
  • 🤷 It's okay to say "I don't know, but..."
  • 👥 Redirect to teammates if needed

For Audience:

  • ❓ Ask genuinely curious questions
  • 🔗 Connect to course concepts
  • 🤝 Be respectful in challenge
  • 💡 "What about..." style questions work
  • 👍 Compliment something specific
