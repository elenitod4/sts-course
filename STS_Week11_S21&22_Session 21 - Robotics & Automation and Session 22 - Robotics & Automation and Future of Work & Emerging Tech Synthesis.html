21 definitions

Key Terms

🤖 Robot

Programmable machine that performs actions autonomously or semi-autonomously.

⚙️ Automation

Technology performing tasks with minimal human input.

🔧 Robotics

Engineering field: design, build, operate robots.

Not all automation is robots. But all robots involve automation.

21 types

Types of Robots

🏭 Industrial

Factory arms: welding, assembly, painting.

🏠 Service

Vacuums, delivery bots, security patrol.

🏥 Medical

Surgery (da Vinci), rehab, prosthetics.

🎖️ Military

Drones, bomb disposal, autonomous vehicles.

👋 Social

Elderly companions, education assistants.

🧍 Humanoid

Boston Dynamics, Tesla Optimus.

21 spectrum

Automation Levels

Level 1-2: Assisted

Human in control. Machine helps with info or simple tasks. (cruise control, autocomplete)

Level 3: Conditional

Machine handles most tasks. Human intervenes when needed. (advanced driver assist)

Level 4: High

Machine operates independently in most situations. (warehouse robots)

Level 5: Full

No human needed. Any situation. (mostly theoretical for general tasks)

21 AI + robotics

When Robots Get AI

Traditional robots

Pre-programmed. Repeat same action. Can't adapt. Factories only.

AI-powered robots

Learn, perceive, decide, adapt. Navigate unknown environments. Improve over time.

21 jobs

Automation & Jobs

Already automated

Manufacturing, customer service bots, warehouse picking, basic data entry, toll collection.

Being automated now

Driving, fast food prep, some medical procedures, security patrol, delivery.

Pattern: repetitive + predictable = automated first. But the boundary keeps moving.

21 three views

Three Views on Automation + Jobs

😊 Optimist

New jobs will emerge that we can't imagine yet. Always happened before.

😰 Pessimist

This time is different. AI + robots = fewer human-only tasks left.

🤔 Realist

Some die, some transform, some emerge. Transition is painful without policy support.

STS answer: it depends on POLICY, not just technology.

🤖💼😅

Robots: "Here to help humans!"

Also robots: "Your job has been automated. Learn to code?"

Tech is not destiny. Policy shapes outcomes.

21 autonomous weapons

Should Robots Kill?

Ban argument

Machines should never decide to kill. No accountability. Lowers threshold for conflict.

Allow argument

Reduces military casualties. More precise than humans. Removes emotion from combat.

Campaign to Stop Killer Robots: pushes for global ban. Debate ongoing at UN.

21 PH context

Robotics in the Philippines

🇵🇭 Current uses

Manufacturing assembly, agricultural drones, telemedicine bots, search & rescue drones.

⚠️ Key concern

BPO sector automation. 1M+ jobs at risk if AI + robotics replace call center work.
