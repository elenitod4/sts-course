16 key lesson

The STS Lens: A Reminder

STS is not: "Here's what this technology does."

STS IS: "Here's what this technology does TO society, who benefits, who's harmed, and what we should do about it."

16 clarification

Paradigm Shift vs Intellectual Revolution

Paradigm Shift

How SCIENCE changes internally (Kuhn). Normal → crisis → revolution within the scientific community.

Intellectual Revolution

How a BIG IDEA changes SOCIETY broadly. Affects culture, politics, identity, worldview.

They overlap. But paradigm shift is about science methodology. Intellectual revolution is about societal impact.
