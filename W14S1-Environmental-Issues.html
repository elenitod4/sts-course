03The Science

Climate Change: The Science

Cause: Enhanced greenhouse effect from excess CO2 & methane

Since Industrial Revolution: CO2 increased 50% (280→420 ppm)

Temperature rise: +1.1°C average global temperature

Sources: Fossil fuels (73%), agriculture (12%), industry (6%)

03Global Impact

Climate Change Impacts: Global

🌊 Sea Level Rise

Sources:

PH Reality:

05Definition

What is E-Waste?

Discarded electronic devices containing toxic materials AND valuable materials.

📱 Phones💻 Laptops📺 TVs🔋 Batteries🖨️ Printers🎮 Consoles

Toxic: Lead, Mercury, Cadmium | Valuable: Gold, Silver, Copper, Rare Earth

05Philippine Context 🇵🇭

E-Waste in the Philippines

📊 ~400,000 metric tons e-waste generated per year

🗑️ Most ends up in landfills or informal recycling

⚖️ RA 9003 (Ecological Solid Waste Management Act) covers e-waste

05The Dark Side

Where Does Your Old Phone Go?

1️⃣ Landfill: Toxic chemicals leach into soil & groundwater

2️⃣ Shipped overseas: To developing countries for informal recycling

3️⃣ Burned: Workers (sometimes children) extract metals by burning

Health effects: Cancer, lung disease, birth defects, neurological damage

Issue 4

DEFORESTATION

Losing Our Lungs

06Global

Deforestation Facts

🌳 10 million hectares cleared per year (size of South Korea)

🐄 Main drivers: cattle ranching, palm oil, soy, logging, urbanization

🌡️ Deforestation contributes ~15% of global carbon emissions

🦎 Tropical forests hold 50% of all species on Earth

06Philippines 🇵🇭

PH Forest Cover Loss

90%

Forest cover in 1900

24%

Forest cover today

Causes: Illegal logging, kaingin (slash-and-burn), mining, urban expansion, commercial plantations

06Why It Matters

Why Deforestation Matters

🌍 Carbon Sinks

Forests absorb CO2. Cut them = more greenhouse gases.

💧 Water Regulation

Prevent floods & landslides. No trees = Ormoc tragedy.

🦜 Biodiversity

50% of species depend on forests. Philippine Eagle endangered.

👥 Indigenous Peoples

14M IPs in PH depend on forests for livelihood & culture.

COMPANIES: "WE PLANTED 1 TREE!" 🌱

🪓🌲🌲🌲🌲

ALSO COMPANIES: *cut 10,000 hectares for business*

Greenwashing: Making destruction look eco-friendly since forever 🌿

07S&T as Problem

Science & Technology: Part of the Problem

  • Industrial Revolution = fossil fuel dependency
  • Planned obsolescence = more e-waste (new phone every year)
  • Intensive agriculture = chemical runoff & soil degradation
  • Transportation tech = vehicle emissions & urban sprawl
  • Mining for tech materials = habitat destruction
07S&T as Solution

Science & Technology: Part of the Solution

  • Renewable energy (solar, wind, hydro, geothermal)
  • Carbon capture & storage technology
  • AI/satellite monitoring for deforestation
  • Biodegradable materials & bioplastics
  • Electric vehicles & green transportation
  • Waste-to-energy conversion
08Summary

Key Takeaways 🔑

  • Climate change: human-caused, PH is 4th most vulnerable
  • Pollution kills 9M/year; PH is 3rd largest ocean plastic source
  • E-waste: 62M tons globally, growing 3-5% annually
  • PH forest cover: 90% (1900) → 24% (today)
  • S&T is both cause and solution — the key is responsible use
