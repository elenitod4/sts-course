02Core Concept

What is Human Flourishing?

Living to one's fullest potential — achieving well-being, meaning, purpose, and fulfillment across ALL dimensions of life.

Not just "being happy" in the moment. It's living a complete, excellent life.

The Greek Master

EUDAIMONIA

"Living well and doing well" — Aristotle (384-322 BCE)

Eastern Views

EASTERN
PERSPECTIVES

Harmony Over Individual Achievement

04Confucianism

Confucian Flourishing: Ren (仁)

Ren (仁) = Humaneness, Benevolence, Goodness

• Flourish by cultivating moral character

• Through proper relationships (Five Relationships)

• Harmony in relationships = harmony in self

• Education and self-cultivation are central

04Buddhism

Buddhist Flourishing: The Middle Path

Flourishing = freedom from suffering (dukkha)

• Middle Path: avoid extremes of indulgence & asceticism

• Eightfold Path → right understanding, intention, speech, action...

• Inner peace, compassion, mindfulness

• Not about accumulating but LETTING GO

04Filipino 🇵🇭

Kaginhawaan (well-being)Kasaganaan (abundance)Kapayapaan (peace)Bayanihan (cooperation)Malasakit (compassion)

You flourish WITH your family and community, never alone. 🇵🇭

Part 2

DIMENSIONS OF
FLOURISHING

A Complete Life Excels Across Multiple Areas

05Dimension 1

Physical Flourishing 💪

Health, nutrition, fitness, freedom from disease, safe living conditions.

Without a healthy body, other forms of flourishing are limited. "Health is wealth" — basic but fundamental. Your body is the vessel for everything else.

05Dimension 2

Intellectual Flourishing 🧠

Education, critical thinking, creativity, curiosity, lifelong learning.

Using your mind to understand the world, solve problems, and create knowledge. This is what you're doing RIGHT NOW as students. Never stop learning.

05Dimension 3

Emotional/Psychological Flourishing 🫀

Mental health, resilience, positive emotions, self-awareness.

Not the absence of negative emotions but the ability to navigate them. Emotional intelligence matters as much as IQ.

05Dimension 4

Social Flourishing 🤝

Meaningful relationships, community belonging, trust, cooperation.

Quality of relationships is the #1 PREDICTOR of life satisfaction (Harvard 85-year study). Isolation kills flourishing.

05Dimension 5

Spiritual/Moral Flourishing ✨

Sense of purpose, meaning, values, connection to something greater.

Can be religious or secular. Having a 'WHY' for your life. Living according to your values with moral integrity.

05Dimension 6

Economic Flourishing 💰

Financial security, meaningful work, fair wages, meeting needs.

Money doesn't buy happiness, but poverty DEFINITELY prevents flourishing. Economic justice enables human potential.

06Flow

Flow State (Csikszentmihalyi)

The state of complete absorption in a challenging activity:

⏰ Time disappears — hours feel like minutes

🧠 Self-awareness fades — you're "in the zone"

📈 Performance peaks — your best work happens here

When do YOU experience flow? Gaming? Coding? Art? Sports?

Part 4

BARRIERS TO
FLOURISHING

What Prevents People From Living Well?

07Systemic

Structural Barriers

  • Poverty & economic inequality
  • Discrimination (race, gender, class, religion)
  • Lack of access to education & healthcare
  • War, conflict, political instability
  • Environmental degradation
  • Corruption & bad governance
07Personal

Individual Barriers

  • Mental health issues (depression, anxiety)
  • Lack of purpose or direction
  • Addiction (substances, social media, gambling)
  • Toxic relationships
  • Fixed mindset & learned helplessness
  • Comparison culture (social media)
